Our Philosophy

Two Traditions,
One Cup.

dsy teahouse was born from a deep reverence for the art of tea — where the mindful, structured Japanese Chanoyu ceremony meets the relaxed, communal Chinese Gongfu style.

Seated on tatami mats, surrounded by low wooden tables and the quiet scent of aged wood, guests are invited to slow down and be present. Every cup is a conversation between two ancient cultures, brewed with intention.

We believe tea is not merely a drink — it is a practice, a pause, a return to what matters.

The Tea & The Space

What Awaits You

☕

Aged Pu'er

Earthy, deep, and complex. Our aged Pu'er is sourced from Yunnan province and pressed into cakes that carry decades of history in every steep.

🍵

Ceremonial Matcha

Stone-ground from first-flush shade-grown leaves. Whisked to a luminous froth in the tradition of Japanese Chanoyu — vivid, grassy, and serene.

🌿

Tieguanyin Oolong

The Iron Goddess of Mercy. A lightly oxidised oolong with a floral, orchid-like fragrance that unfolds across multiple infusions.

🚪

Private Tatami Rooms

Reserve an intimate tatami room for a personal tea session. Ideal for quiet reflection, small gatherings, or a guided ceremony experience.

🧘

Meditation Corner

A dedicated space for stillness. Sit, breathe, and let the ambient sounds of the teahouse guide you into a moment of genuine calm.

📜

Guided Tea Ceremonies

Join one of our hosted sessions where our tea masters walk you through the ritual, history, and sensory nuances of each tradition.
